Drupal is a powerful open-source content management system (CMS) designed for building flexible, scalable, and secure websites. Known for its robust architecture, strong content modeling capabilities, and extensive module ecosystem, Drupal is widely used by developers and organizations that require highly customizable platforms. It’s commonly used for enterprise websites, government portals, universities, and complex digital platforms where performance, security, and structured content management are essential.
